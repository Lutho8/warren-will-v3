// 01

BIO

Warren Will is a Munich-based DJ and producer playing groovy, bass-driven Tech House — the kind that fills floors and keeps them full. From basement clubs to open-air mountain stages across Germany, Austria and Switzerland, his sets are built for one thing: the moment the whole room moves.

Dad of two, relentless digger, and the man behind the Vol mix series — including Vol2 · Berlin Techno. His remix of Ace of Base's "All That She Wants" and the free-download VIP "She Wants To Move" have become staples of his sets, alongside live recordings like the Graf Rumford Afro House mix and the Howler live series.
